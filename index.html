Who are we ?


We at Gopalam Gems & Jewellery collection have a wide range of exquisitely crafted designs, created to enhance every look and light up every occasion.
We offer complete range of silver jewellery, ranging from rings, earrings, pendants, necklaces, cufflings and bracelets setted with Precious and semi precious stones.
The remarkable colour, lustre and aesthetic appearance of stones add more value to jewellery.

What We Do ?


We ensure that each of our products are the specimen of quality and style in its line. We always try to match with all the requirements of our customers. Owing to our commitment and dedication, we have been able to gain the confidence of our existing clients.

We also offer a complete range of “Garnet” semi-precious gemstone. The range comprises of finely cut polished shapes - oval, cushion, round, pear, fancy. This collection can be used for making jewellery, rings or other ornaments.

More About us?


We aim at revolutionizing real jewellery and lifestyle scenario with a firm focus on craftsmanship, quality and user experience, also offering beautifully designed jewellery.
We take careful note of prevailing jewellery trends. All our works are masterpieces and symbolize beauty, magnificence and charm of jewellery and fashion accessories.
We believe in lifelong relationships and constantly work to ensure it
